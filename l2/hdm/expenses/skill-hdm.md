# skill-hdm.md — Expense Reports (HDM-18)

## Module summary
Employee expense reimbursement: travel, meals, fuel, accommodation.
Employees submit with receipts, managers approve, finance reimburses.

## Clarification questions
- Per-diem policy or actual expense reimbursement?
- Category limits — max per meal, per hotel night?
- Receipt required for all amounts or only above threshold?
- Corporate card reconciliation needed?
- Integration with travel booking system?

## Features
- core: expense policies, expense reports with line items, approval flow
- optional: per-diem calculation by destination
- optional: corporate card statement reconciliation
- optional: mileage/fuel reimbursement by km
- optional: multi-currency for international travel

## KPIs
- Expense volume by category and department
- Average report approval time
- Rejection rate and top rejection reasons
- Employees with open reports older than 30 days
