# skill-hdm.md — CRM (HDM-08)

## Module summary
Manages commercial relationships: leads, pipeline, accounts, and interaction
history. Provides 360° customer view. Integrates with sales for orders.

## Clarification questions
- Sales pipeline stages — how many, what defines each?
- Lead source tracking needed (marketing attribution)?
- Multiple contacts per company account?
- Activity logging — calls, emails, visits — automatic or manual?
- Integration with marketing automation for lead nurturing?
- LGPD consent management required?

## Features
- core: lead, account, opportunity pipeline, activity log
- optional: lead scoring
- optional: LGPD consent tracking
- optional: email integration (sync sent/received)
- optional: integration with marketing automation (HDM-08b)

## KPIs
- Pipeline by stage (count and value)
- Win rate (won / total closed)
- Average sales cycle duration
- Activity volume by rep
- Lead conversion rate (lead → opportunity → won)
- Overdue activities

Generate: 20 leads in various stages, 15 accounts, 12 opportunities across
pipeline stages, 30 activities. Ensure funnel is visible with mix of won/lost.
