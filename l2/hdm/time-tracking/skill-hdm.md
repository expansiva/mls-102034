# skill-hdm.md — Time Tracking and Attendance (HDM-10)

## Module summary
Records employee hours, absences, overtime, and shift scheduling.
Feeds payroll with hours data. Brazil REP compliance available as plugin.

## Clarification questions
- Clock-in method — manual entry, mobile app, biometric, REP?
- Shift scheduling needed or fixed hours only?
- Bank of hours or overtime payment?
- Integration with payroll for automatic hour transfer?
- Brazil REP (electronic time recorder) compliance required?

## Features
- core: time entries, absence records, shift assignment
- optional: overtime bank of hours management
- optional: shift scheduling calendar
- optional: manager approval for overtime
- plugin-rep-brazil: REP integration and AFD file generation

## KPIs
- Attendance rate by department
- Overtime hours by employee and department
- Absence frequency and causes
- Bank of hours balance per employee
