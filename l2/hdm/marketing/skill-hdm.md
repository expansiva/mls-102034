# skill-hdm.md — Marketing Automation (HDM-08b)

## Module summary
Extends CRM with campaign automation, email sequences, lead scoring,
and LGPD/GDPR consent management.

## Clarification questions
- Email only or multi-channel (SMS, WhatsApp)?
- Lead scoring — behavior-based, profile-based, or both?
- LGPD consent capture and double opt-in required?
- Integration with existing email provider (SendGrid, SES) or built-in?
- Unsubscribe and preference center needed?

## Features
- core: campaigns, email sequences, consent records
- optional: lead scoring automation
- optional: landing page and form builder
- optional: UTM tracking and attribution
- optional: A/B testing for email content

## KPIs
- Email open rate and click rate by campaign
- Unsubscribe rate
- Lead source attribution (which campaign generated most leads)
- Consent coverage (% of contacts with valid consent)
- Sequence completion rate

Generate: 5 campaigns in various statuses, 3 email sequences, 100 consent
records (mix granted/revoked), campaign performance metrics.
