# skill-hdm.md — Quality Management QMS (HDM-14)

## Module summary
Inspection checklists, non-conformance records, corrective/preventive
actions, and supplier quality. Supports ISO 9001 compliance workflows.

## Clarification questions
- Which inspection points — inbound (goods receipt), production, outbound?
- NCR workflow — who opens, who analyses, who closes?
- CAPA tracking needed?
- Supplier quality scoring linked to purchasing?
- ISO 9001 or other certification compliance required?
- Customer complaint handling included?

## Features
- core: inspection checklists, inspection records, non-conformances
- optional: corrective and preventive actions (CAPA)
- optional: supplier quality rating (integrates with HDM-02)
- optional: customer complaint workflow
- optional: quality dashboard for ISO audits

## KPIs
- NCR count by severity, source, and period
- Average time to close NCR
- Corrective action overdue rate
- Supplier defect rate (PPM)
- First pass yield (production inspections passed without NCR)
